I am a junior year undergraduate and currently pursuing a major in Production Engineering from the National Institute of Technology at Tiruchirappalli, India. I am deeply interested in Machine Learning and occasionally contributing to Open source software. I am accustomed to working on Linux operating systems. Being a part of the university’s coding club (Delta Force) blended in me spirit of teamwork. As a human, I strongly believe in the teachings of Gautam Buddha who says
- Be kind to all, this is the true religion .

Other things in life that I enjoy are Cricket, Chess, Spanish guitar, watching TED/PyCon talks with a cup of Coffee and blogging occasionally.

Last summer, I contributed to coala project under Python Software Foundation being a Google Summer of Code intern and conducted a training session on a Guide to make an open source contribution at EuroPython, Bilbao, Spain.
